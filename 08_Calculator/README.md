 and M
. Your task is to compute and print the results of the following operations:

N+M
N−M

N÷M
 (integer division)

A single line containing two integers N
 and M
 (1≤N,M≤1000)
.

Output
Print five lines in the format as shown in the example.

Example
InputCopy
6 4
OutputCopy
